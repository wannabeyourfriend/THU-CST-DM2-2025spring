#### PT图求解

绘制PT图并进行拓扑排序
$$
1 \to 3 \to 2 \to 4 \to 7 \to 5 \to 6 \to 8 \to 10 \to 9
$$
![image-20250407091617672](assets/image-20250407091617672.png)
$$
\text{From } 2 \to n:\\
p(v_j) = \text{max}_{v_i∈prev(v_j)}[p(v_i) + w(v_i, v_j)]\\
\text{From } (n-1) \to 1:\\
t(v_j) = \text{min}_{v_i∈next(v_j)}(t(v_i) - w(v_j, v_i))\\
$$
